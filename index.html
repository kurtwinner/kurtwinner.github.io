Safe Streets for Squirrels

Squirrels Use Streets Too!

  • about
  • roadkill

Every day, thousands of squirrels needlessly die on the streets of Portland.
It doesn't have to be this way.
Drivers and cyclists could use more caution and watch out for animals darting out in front of them while on the road.

A recent driver tells us, "I didn't even see it until it was too late, I feel horrible."

Drivers and cyclists are advised to slow down 5 miles per hour in neighborhoods where wildlife is abundant.

Thanks for your support.
