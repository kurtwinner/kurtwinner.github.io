  • And, for creatures larger than squirrels...

Sadly, sometimes crossing systems fail, or our furry friends ignore crossings in their hurry. When that happens, you may have an injured squirrel.

As wildlife, in theory they are the responsibility of your wildlife agency, but rarely will agencies respond.

In that case, you must, if you wish to care for our damaged furry friends, convey them to a veterinary clinic for treatment, or take care of Squirrel yourself, both at your own expense.

If Squirrel is beyond help, you now have some Roadkill which can, waste not, want not, be a tasty treat, if your area allows.

Roadkill laws vary in each state, province, and territory; please consult your area wildlife agency, or you can use this guide.
